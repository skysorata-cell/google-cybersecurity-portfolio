## Priority Recommendations

### 1. Implement Data Encryption
- Encrypt cardholder data at rest and in transit
- Store sensitive data in a secure environment
- Critical for PCI DSS and GDPR compliance

### 2. Enforce Least Privilege & Separation of Duties
- Limit access based on job roles
- Reduce insider threat and breach impact

### 3. Strengthen Password Policies
- Enforce minimum length and complexity
- Align with PCI DSS requirements

### 4. Improve Data Classification & Privacy Controls
- Inventory and classify sensitive data
- Apply consistent privacy handling procedures

---

## Defensive Improvements

### 5. Deploy Intrusion Detection System (IDS)
- Monitor traffic and detect anomalies
- Enable alerting for suspicious activity

### 6. Centralize Password Management
- Reduce password fatigue
- Improve enforcement and security

### 7. Formalize Legacy System Monitoring
- Establish scheduled reviews and maintenance
- Document intervention procedures

### 8. Create Disaster Recovery & Backup Plans
- Implement regular data backups
- Ensure business continuity during incidents
